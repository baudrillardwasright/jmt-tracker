Daily figures are estimates from satellite check-ins snapped to the route: miles are camp-to-camp along the trail, gain and loss come from the route's elevation profile, and hours run from the day's first to last check-in — days with a single check-in show no hours.

Positions are sent from a ZOLEO satellite communicator, so updates arrive only when a check-in is sent — gaps of a day or more are normal and expected. This page updates itself; nobody is typing these in. Map data © OpenStreetMap contributors, tiles by OpenTopoMap.
